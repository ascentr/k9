Why go Raw

Dogs are carnivors, that would eat raw food in their natural habitat. Their bodies are designed to eat raw foods and not consume processed grain based product with addatives. Normal commercial dog food consists of 50% processed ingredients.

We Select high quality raw feed for dogs sourced from leading brands priortising the canine health and happiness.

Benefits of Raw Feeding

  • Strengthened immune system.
  • Improved skin and softer, shinier coat.
  • Superior dental health.
  • Superior joint and bone health.
  • Reduced stool volume and odour.
  • Enhanced reproductive health.
  • Reduced Body Odour.
